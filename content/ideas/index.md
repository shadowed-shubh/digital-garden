# Ideas

Ideas are projects that are not fully scoped or built yet. They stay here until they become active projects.

## Current Ideas

- [[ideas/Hyprland Phone Integration|Hyprland Phone Integration]]
- [[projects/AI Companion in Go|AI Companion in Go]] is currently active, but it also started as an idea around local companion AI and Go learning.

## Idea Criteria

An idea should become a project when it has:

- a clear v1
- a repo or implementation path
- a reason it teaches something important
- a way to demo or measure progress
